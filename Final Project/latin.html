Origins of Latin Music

Latin music is a vast range of musical styles and genres originating from Latin America, Spain, and Protugal, as well as music produced by the latino population in the United States and Canada. This genre blends indigenous, African, and European, particularly spanish influences. Key elements include rich percussion, polyrhythms, and genres like salsa, cumbia, reggaeton, bachata, and merengue.
