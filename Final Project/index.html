    Evolution

    Throughout time, music has changed and taken many different forms. Now, music is commongly sorted into different genres or styles of music. Some styles originated from strong historical reasoning or events, and others simply developed with the change in style and culture as time passed. Ethnicity and culture has also shaped music strongly in multiple parts of the world, and all music is exchanged globally. Music is one of the most important and popular ways humans have expressed themselves, whether it is their emotions or their values or beliefs, and will continue to do so as it impacts many people around the world.
