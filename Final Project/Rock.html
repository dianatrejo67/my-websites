Rock music arose in the 1940's. It evolved by mixing multiple genres together, such as R&B, blues, and swing music, and is heavily influenced by the African American music that came before it. It was characterized by electronic instruments, such as guitars, and rebellious lyrics. Today rock has many subgenres and has influenced many fashion cultures, and has evolved a lot since the 1940's.
