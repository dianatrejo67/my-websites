The Origins of Christmas

Diana Trejo



Origin of Christmas

Christmas originated from early European winter solstice celebrations, adopted and Christianized by the Roman church in the 4th century to coincide with the pagan festivals of Saturnalia and the birthday of Sol Invictus (the Unconquered Sun) on December 25th



The Christian meaning

Beyond the literal birth of Jesus, Christmas holds several profound meanings for Catholics, primarily centered on the incarnation, the concept of God becoming man to redeem humanity. It is a celebration of divine love, salvation, and the start of Jesus' life on earth which would culminate in his death and resurrection
